Our Business

We specialize in the sale and import/export of high-quality chemical products manufactured by leading domestic Japanese manufacturers, serving clients across the food, cosmetics, and industrial sectors. We place the highest priority on integrity in every transaction and the cultivation of long-term partnerships.

I

Procurement & Stable Supply

Strong partnerships with major Japanese manufacturers ensure reliable sourcing.

We maintain a continuous supply of raw materials for food, cosmetics, and industrial use, with systems in place to prevent supply disruptions.

II

Quality Control

Strict quality verification from the point of procurement.

We handle only products that meet manufacturer specifications and respond to quality-related inquiries with integrity and speed.

III

Technical Documents & Samples

We provide full technical assistance during your product evaluation and development phases.

Long-Term Partnership

Message from the President

President's Message

President & CEO
Naoyuki Kashiwabara
Sansho Chemical Products Co., Ltd.

Since our founding in 1969, Sansho Chemical Products Co., Ltd. has operated as a specialty trading company in the chemical sector, conducting every transaction with sincerity and placing the highest priority on building lasting relationships of trust with our clients both in Japan and abroad.

Our mission is to support the continued, stable production activities of our clients by ensuring a reliable supply of high-quality chemical raw materials. At every stage — procurement, quality control, and technical information provision — we maintain an uncompromising standard of integrity.

Even as market conditions evolve, we are committed to maintaining strong ties with leading Japanese manufacturers to uphold the stability of supply and the reliability of product quality. We firmly believe that long-term partnerships with our clients define the very purpose of our business.

Together as a company, we are dedicated to meeting the trust and expectations placed in us. We look forward to the opportunity to serve you and to grow together over the long term.
